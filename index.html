About ColorStack

Our Mission

ColorStack is a national organization dedicated to increasing the representation of Black, Latinx, and Native American students in computing graduate programs and tech industry leadership positions.

WashU Chapter

Our chapter at Washington University in St. Louis brings together students passionate about technology and creating inclusive spaces in computing. We focus on academic support, professional development, and building a strong community.
